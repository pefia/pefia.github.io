Our story

Who We Are

Ace Plumbing & Heating started in 2013 with one van, one engineer and a simple promise: treat every home like our own. More than a decade on, that promise hasn't changed.

We're a small, tight-knit team of Gas-Safe registered engineers who actually live and work in East London. When you call, you speak to a real person — and the same friendly faces turn up at your door.

No call-centres, no faceless contractors, no surprise bills. Just honest, skilled plumbers who take pride in getting it right the first time.
