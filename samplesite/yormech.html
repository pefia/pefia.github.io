Good to know

Common Questions

Do I need to be home when you work on the car?

Not necessarily. As long as we can safely access the vehicle and you're reachable by phone, many customers are at work while Faznan completes the job.

Is the £95 full service really the final price?

Yes. For standard services, the price is fixed and agreed before we start. If an unexpected issue is found during the service, Faznan will call you first. We never proceed with additional work without your approval.

How quickly can you get to me?

Often same day, especially for MOT collection and urgent repairs. Availability varies — just send a WhatsApp and Faznan will give you an honest timeframe.

Do you cover my car's make and model?

We cover all makes and models — petrol, diesel, and mild hybrid. If you have a fully electric vehicle, get in touch and we'll advise.

What about a warranty on your work?

Yes. We offer a warranty on repairs for your peace of mind. Details discussed at time of booking.

What if my car won't start?

Diagnostics are one of our core services. Message via WhatsApp with your car make, model, registration, and symptoms and Faznan will advise.
