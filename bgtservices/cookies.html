Cookie Policy

What cookies we use and how to control them.

Last updated: June 2026

What cookies we use

We use cookies ONLY for Google Analytics (_ga, _gid). These are anonymous traffic tracking cookies only.

No personal data cookies

We do NOT use cookies to track you personally, store preferences, or collect personal information.

What Google Analytics cookies do

These cookies help us understand how visitors use our website — which pages are most popular, how long people stay, and where they come from. This information is entirely anonymous and cannot be used to identify you personally.

Your options

You can disable cookies in your browser settings anytime. You can also opt-out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Cookie list

_ga — Google Analytics. Expires after 2 years. Used to distinguish users.

_gid — Google Analytics. Expires after 24 hours. Used to distinguish users.
