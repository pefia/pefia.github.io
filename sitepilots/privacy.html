Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

SitePilots (“we”, “us”, “our”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what personal data we collect, how we use it, and the rights you have under UK data protection law (the UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018).

Who we are

SitePilots designs and supports websites for UK trades and local service businesses. For any privacy question, contact us at sitepilots@outlook.com.

Information we collect

  • Contact details you provide — such as your name, email address, phone number and message — when you enquire or request a quote.
  • Project information you share with us so we can scope and build your website.
  • Technical data such as basic, anonymised usage information needed to keep the site secure and working.

How we use your information

  • To respond to enquiries and provide quotes.
  • To design, build, host and support your website.
  • To manage our agreement with you and process payments.
  • To meet our legal and accounting obligations.

Lawful basis

We rely on legitimate interests (responding to your enquiry), contract (delivering the services you ask for), and legal obligation (record-keeping) as our lawful bases for processing.

Sharing your data

We do not sell your data. We only share it with trusted service providers who help us operate — for example hosting and email providers — and only as far as needed to deliver our service.

Retention

We keep personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes above, or as required by law, after which it is securely deleted.

Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, delete or restrict the use of your personal data, and to object to processing. To exercise any right, email sitepilots@outlook.com. You may also complain to the Information Commissioner’s Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

Security

We use appropriate technical and organisational measures to keep your data safe, including encryption in transit and access controls.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will always be published on this page.
